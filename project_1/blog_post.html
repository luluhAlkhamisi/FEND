Where Did Coffee Originate?

Where did coffee originate? Well, that’s the easy bit. At the very beginning, it came from Ethiopia.
But how the bean made it to every corner of the globe? That’s what we are going to dig into.
After a slow discovery in Africa, coffee went west into Europe to be discovered and coveted by
the newer civilizations as well as east into Asia where it was planted and harvested.
